# Corporate Strategy Roundup - 2025-11-14

Here's a summary of notable partnerships, acquisitions, and strategic moves by top AI vendors in 2024-2025:

**Microsoft:**
*   Acquired OpenAI's commercial business unit for $25 billion (late 2024).
*   Invested significantly in OpenAI (totaling ~$14 billion).
*   Plans to invest $80 billion in AI data centers in FY2025.
*   Acquired Inflection AI's talent and licensed its technology.
*   Integrated ChatGPT into products like Bing, Azure, Edge, and Microsoft 365 Copilot.

**ServiceNow:**
*   Acquired Moveworks ($2.85 billion, March 2025) to enhance generative AI agents.
*   Acquired Logik.ai (April 2025) to expand AI automation into sales workflows.

**AMD:**
*   Acquired AI software startup MK1 (November 2025) for performance and efficiency.
*   Acquired ZT Systems (2025) to develop rack-scale AI solutions with Instinct GPUs.
*   Inked a 6-gigawatt deal for AI infrastructure with OpenAI.

**MongoDB:**
*   Acquired Voyage AI ($220 million, March 2025) to enhance native search and vector capabilities for GenAI and RAG systems.

**HP:**
*   Acquired wearable AI hardware startup Humane ($116 million, February 2025) to enter the AI-native device market and formed "HP IQ" innovation lab.

**IBM:**
*   Acquired Hakkoda Inc. (April 2025) to strengthen cloud consulting.
*   Acquired HashiCorp ($6.4 billion) to consolidate cloud-native AI tools.
*   Partnered with Anthropic to make Claude models available in IBM's IDE.

**Nvidia:**
*   Acquired Gretel.ai (over $320 million) for synthetic data.
*   Partners with HPE and Oracle to enhance AI performance and enterprise AI adoption.

**OpenAI:**
*   Acquired Rockset (2024) for real-time analytics.
*   Acquired Jony Ive's hardware startup, io ($6.5 billion).
*   Agreed to purchase Windsurf AI coding tool ($3 billion).
*   Struck a $300 billion cloud agreement for AI infrastructure with Oracle.

**Amazon Web Services (AWS):**
*   Deepened collaboration with Anthropic, including a $4 billion investment (November 2024), making Anthropic its primary training partner.

**Google:**
*   Invested up to $2 billion in Anthropic.
*   Added AI models from Meta and Anthropic to its cloud platform.

**Other Notable Moves:**
*   **Apple** acquired over 30 AI startups in 2023.
*   **DataRobot** acquired Agnostiq (February 2025) for agentic AI application development.
*   **Thomson Reuters** acquired Materia and Casetext.
*   **CoreWeave** acquired Core Scientific for $9 billion.
*   **Meta** acquired AI voice cloning startup PlayAI.
*   **Perplexity AI** partnered with 1Password, PayPal, and T-Mobile.
*   **Anthropic** made Claude models available in Apple's Xcode 26.
