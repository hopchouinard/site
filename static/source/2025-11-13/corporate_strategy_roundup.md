# Corporate Strategy Roundup - 2025-11-13

Here's a summary of notable partnerships, acquisitions, and strategic moves by top AI vendors from 2023 into 2025:

**Microsoft:**
*   **Acquisitions:** Inflection AI (for conversational AI), Nuance Communications ($19.7B for conversational AI).
*   **Partnerships:** Deepened collaboration with OpenAI (investment growing to ~$14B, holding ~27% stake, exclusive IP rights and Azure API exclusivity until AGI declared), partnered with Meta (Llama 2 AI on Azure/Windows), collaborated with Nscale, NVIDIA, and Start Campus (deploying 12,600 NVIDIA GPUs in Portugal for AI computational capacity).
*   **Strategic Moves:** Integrating AI across Microsoft 365, introduced Azure AI and Copilot AI.

**Amazon:**
*   **Acquisitions:** Covariant (AI for robotics in logistics), Perceive (low-power AI for consumer products like Echo/Ring).
*   **Partnerships:** Collaborated with IBM (AI governance for SageMaker), Nvidia (generative AI guardrails), integrated Anthropic Claude 3 and Amazon Titan into media offerings.
*   **Strategic Moves:** $38B deal with OpenAI (running generative AI workloads on AWS, including Nvidia GPUs), invested $8B in Anthropic (making AWS primary training partner, leveraging Trainium2 chips). Dual strategy: supporting OpenAI with Nvidia GPUs on AWS and Anthropic with custom Trainium2 chips.

**Nvidia:**
*   **Acquisitions:** Over 25 companies to become a full-stack AI provider, including Run:AI (AI/ML optimization), Deci AI (AI model deployment), Shoreline.io (cloud management), Bright Computing (cluster management), Mellanox Technologies (data centers/networking).
*   **Partnerships:** Major cloud providers (Microsoft Azure, Google Cloud, AWS, Oracle Cloud) for AI applications.
*   **Strategic Moves:** "Circular deals" (investing in customers/partners who commit to GPU purchases), positioning to control critical AI ecosystem components, investing in a "multitrillion-dollar AI future."

**Google:**
*   **Acquisitions:** Phiar Technologies (AI for autonomous vehicles), Mantis AI (AI chatbots), DeepMind Technologies (general-purpose learning algorithms), Wiz ($32B for cloud security in 2025).
*   **Investments:** Enable (AI-powered financial tools).
*   **Partnerships:** Google Cloud partnered with Elasticsearch (big data with generative AI).
*   **Strategic Moves:** Developing "Project Suncatcher" (solar-powered satellites with TPUs), integrating real-time prediction market data, introduced Ironwood TPU (seventh-gen AI chip), Google Cloud focusing on nurturing emerging AI startups.

**OpenAI:**
*   **Acquisitions:** Rockset (real-time analytics database), Multi (video calling platform, acquihire), Software Applications Incorporated (macOS app Sky), io Products ($6.5B, design startup by Jony Ive, signaling move into hardware), Statsig ($1.1B to scale products), Global Illumination (augmented reality).
*   **Partnerships:** Microsoft (significant investment), Reddit, G42 (advanced AI solutions in UAE), Stack Overflow (enhancing models), Amazon Web Services ($38B for cloud computing/Nvidia GPUs), Oracle ($30B cloud deal, $300B five-year commitment), Broadcom ($10B for custom AI chips).
*   **Strategic Moves:** Evolving into a full-fledged technology ecosystem, aiming to own every layer of the AI experience, building stability across multiple cloud platforms, preparing for potential IPO.
