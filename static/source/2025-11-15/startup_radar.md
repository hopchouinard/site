**Key Open-Source Agentic AI Frameworks:**

*   **LangChain and LangGraph:** Pioneering frameworks for building LLM applications with composable components. LangGraph offers a graph-based approach for more controlled workflows.
*   **CrewAI:** Specializes in orchestrating multiple specialized agents for collaborative workflows and complex problem-solving.
*   **AutoGen:** Developed by Microsoft, focuses on autonomous, asynchronous agent collaboration.
*   **Agno, SmolAgents, Mastra, Pydantic AI, and Atomic Agents:** Newer frameworks enhancing LLM functionality for knowledge acquisition, data access, and autonomous action.
*   **Kagent and Dapr Agents:** Focus on operational resilience for AI agent frameworks, integrating with cloud-native infrastructure and microservices.
*   **XAgent:** Known for autonomous planning and execution with a hierarchical task planning framework.
*   **MetaGPT:** Features innovative multi-agent collaboration for sophisticated communication and coordination.
