# Corporate Strategy Roundup - 2025-11-10

Here's a summary of notable partnerships, acquisitions, and strategic moves by top AI vendors in 2023 and 2024:

*   **Microsoft:** Significantly invested in OpenAI (reportedly $13 billion total, including a $10 billion investment in 2023 and acquiring OpenAI's commercial unit for $25 billion in 2024). Also invested in Mistral AI and acquired Inflection AI for $650 million, integrating its personnel and technology. Microsoft is focusing on AI infrastructure, tooling vendors, and hardware makers.
*   **Google:** Acquired Mantis AI, Phiar Technologies, and Kaggle in 2023. Made substantial investments in DeepMind Health and Anthropic (up to $2 billion). Reportedly acquired Hugging Face for $10 billion. Google Cloud is actively partnering with emerging AI startups and Mistral AI.
*   **Amazon:** Invested $8 billion in Anthropic and has a $38 billion deal with OpenAI, leveraging AWS cloud services. Acquired AI robotics company Covariant and Perceive. Collaborated with IBM for AI governance and partnered with Nvidia.
*   **Nvidia:** Invested heavily in AI startups, including CoreWeave, Kore.ai, xAI, OpenAI, Cohere, Mistral, and Perplexity. Acquired Run:ai ($700 million), Shoreline.io ($100 million), and Deci AI ($300 million), focusing on "core AI" companies.
*   **IBM:** Plans to acquire HashiCorp for $6.4 billion and purchased Software AG's StreamSets and webMethods platforms for $2.3 billion to enhance its hybrid cloud and AI offerings. Acquired Seek AI to enable natural language querying of enterprise data. Partnered with Meta (AI Alliance), Mistral AI, and Anthropic.
